ViecVui operating system — 7 internal functions
and the timeline to place 50 students in Australia

This is the view from inside: the seven functions ViecVui runs itself, what each does across the phases, and how they hand off to one another. Students, training providers, Australian employers, the migration agent, COLAB and investors are the external parties each function works with — they are not operating units.

02 · Two-layer model

Inside does the work — Outside is coordinated

Keep the two layers separate: the student is the output (external); the internal unit handling them is Admissions. Technology and Marketing are internal functions too — not external parties.
